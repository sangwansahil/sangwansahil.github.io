About

Dr Virender Singh Sangwan is the Paul Dubord Chair in Cornea & Anterior Segment in LV Prasad Eye Institute(LVPEI),Banjara Hills Hyderabad. His areas of specialization include stem cells, corneal diseases, ocular surface disorders, cataract surgery, uveitis and ocular allergy. He is the recepient of the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Award (2006) in Medical Sciences, National Technology award(2007) and Sun Pharma Science Award for medical research in 2016. Virender Singh Sangwan completed his medical graduate degree from Maharshi Dayanand University, Haryana, in 1986 and MS Ophthalmology in 1991. He did a fellowship in Cornea & Anterior Segment at L V Prasad Eye Institute, and another in Immunology & Uveitis Services at Harvard Medical School, USA. Now he is Director of Cornea & Anterior Segment, Ocular Immunology & Uveitis Services at L V Prasad Eye Institute, Hyderabad. He is also Adjunct Associate Professor of Ophthalmology, University of Rochester, New York, USA. Read More..
